Peeno's Great Adventure

Peeno's Great Adventure was a project I worked on as part of my Level 3 year 1 game development course in the end of 2024 and start of 2025. The task was to build a proof of concept for a new 3D platformer titled: Peeno's Great Adventure.

I decided to theme my project around the idea of time and seasons passing. To achieve this, I split the level into 4 sections. Spring, Summer, Autumn and Winter. Each season would have its own colour palette and scenery would change over the course of the year to show the passage of time. I decided to have one minigame per season. This would give the player enough time to experience each season and would set a good pace for the game and the different minigames would make it a fun and engaging game.
